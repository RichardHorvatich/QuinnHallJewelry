About Me

-Get to know me-

My name is Quinn Hall. I am a local of Flagstaff, AZ. I have been designing jewlery for two years. Wire wrapping jewelry is my passion. I love designing pieces that touch the heart and soul. I often frequent downtown areas such as Heritage Square, Wheeler Park, Bushmaster Park. If you are not a local and wish to purchase any of my pieces, click the store page and make your purchase with the click of a button. If you have any questions/comments please fill out the contact form at the bottom of the page and I will get back to you. Aside from jewlery I love art, music and festivals.

Thanks for stopping by.
